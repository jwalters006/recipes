Ingredients:
 - 2 slices of cheese
 - 1 tablespoon of butter
 - 2 slices of bread

 Directions:
 1. Heat up a pan on gas stove to medium intensity for 90 seconds.
 2. Spread half a tablespoon of butter on 1 side of each slice of bread.
 3. Place one slice of bread on heated pan, buttered side down.
 4. Place the two slices of cheese on top of the bread slice in the pan.
 5. Place the other slice of bread on top of the cheese slices, buttered
 	side up.
 6. When bottom slice of bread is sufficiently browned, flip sandwich in
 	the pan.
 7. When the other slice of bread is sufficiently browned, turn off heat,
 	remove sandwich, and enjoy.
